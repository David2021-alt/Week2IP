The Akan Naming Convnetion

This website tries to bring information about a Ghanian system of naming their children. children are named according to their day of birth. The first name is usually a day name which corresponds with the day the child was born. E.g for Male children the naming would be as follows

Sunday: Kwasi Monday:Kwadwo, Tuesday: Kwabena, Wedneday:Kwaku, Thursday: Yaw, Friday: Kofi and Saturday: Kwame.

, Else If For females, the names would be as follows;

Sunday: Akosua, Monday: Adwoa, Tuesday: Abena,Wedneday:Akua, Thursday: Yaa; Friday: Afua; Saturday: Ama. These names spread across the country of Ghana.
